Traveler
#} {#
#} {#

#} {# In my spare time, I enjoy traveling, especially doing something really special and interesting. During my second summer in college, I went to Nepal as a science instructor volunteer for a primary shool in Kathmandu. My students are super hard-working, smart and cute. During weekdays, I teach them math, biology and chemistry. On weekends, they lead me to local market, temple and some sweets shop and introduce their religions and culture to me. It was such an amazing experience that I would never forget!#} {#
